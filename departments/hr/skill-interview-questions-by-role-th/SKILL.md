# Thai Interview Questions by Role

Generate practical interview questions that are easy for Thai teams to use.

## Output requirements
- Write in Thai
- Keep questions practical and job-relevant
- Avoid vague or overly theoretical questions
- Mix role-fit, behavioral, and situational questions

## Working method
1. Identify the role and hiring goal
2. Map the most important competencies
3. Create questions that reveal real working behavior
4. Group questions into usable sections
5. Add optional probing follow-up questions if useful

## Default output structure
1. interview objective
2. core question list
3. follow-up questions
4. what to listen for
