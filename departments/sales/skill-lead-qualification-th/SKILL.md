# Thai Lead Qualification

Turn rough lead information into a usable Thai qualification summary.

## Output requirements
- Write in Thai
- Keep the summary easy to scan
- Separate facts from assumptions
- Highlight fit, urgency, and next step
- Do not pretend to know budget, authority, or timeline if not provided

## Working method
1. Extract available lead facts
2. Identify need, possible use case, and buying signals
3. Assess fit level based on provided information only
4. Note missing information that sales should ask next
5. Recommend a practical next step

## Default output structure
1. lead summary
2. qualification status
3. buying signals
4. missing information
5. suggested next step

## Tone guidance
Use plain Thai suitable for sales teams and business owners.
