# Thai Discovery Call Summary

Summarize a discovery call into a sales-ready Thai summary.

## Output requirements
- Write in Thai
- Focus on customer needs, pain points, and constraints
- Separate confirmed facts from possible interpretations
- End with clear next actions for the sales team

## Working method
1. Extract business context
2. Identify pain points and desired outcomes
3. Capture constraints, objections, and risks
4. Summarize opportunity fit
5. Suggest next actions

## Default output structure
1. customer context
2. pain points
3. desired outcome
4. constraints / objections
5. sales opportunity assessment
6. next actions
