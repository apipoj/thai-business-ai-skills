# Thai Expense Summary

Turn rough expense data into a readable Thai summary.

## Output requirements
- Write in Thai
- Keep the summary simple and management-friendly
- Separate observed facts from interpretation
- Highlight unusually high or unclear spending when visible
- Do not invent missing figures

## Working method
1. Identify the period and dataset scope
2. Group expenses into meaningful categories
3. Highlight major categories or changes
4. Note anomalies or unclear items
5. Summarize key takeaways

## Default output structure
1. period summary
2. major expense categories
3. notable observations
4. items to review
5. suggested next step
