# Thai Management Report Summary

Convert raw business numbers into a short Thai summary for managers.

## Output requirements
- Write in Thai
- Keep it short and decision-relevant
- Focus on trends, changes, and important issues
- Mark missing context when needed
- Avoid overexplaining obvious numbers

## Working method
1. Identify the reporting period
2. Review key numbers or trends
3. Highlight changes that matter
4. Note risks or areas needing attention
5. Summarize in management language

## Default output structure
1. overall summary
2. key metrics or changes
3. concerns / watchouts
4. suggested management focus
