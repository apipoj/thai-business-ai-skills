# Thai Weekly Management Update

Turn scattered weekly notes into a clean manager update.

## Output requirements
- Write in Thai
- Keep it concise and easy to scan
- Focus on progress, blockers, risks, and next priorities
- Avoid turning routine detail into noise

## Working method
1. Identify the reporting period and scope
2. Extract major progress items
3. Capture blockers or risks
4. Identify next-week priorities
5. Format for leadership reading

## Default output structure
1. weekly summary
2. progress this week
3. blockers / risks
4. priorities next week
5. support needed
