# Thai 30-Day Content Calendar

Create a practical Thai content plan for a month.

## Output requirements
- Write in Thai
- Keep ideas realistic for small teams
- Balance educational, promotional, trust-building, and engagement content
- Avoid repetitive or overly generic suggestions

## Working method
1. Identify business type and target audience
2. Define content goals
3. Mix content types across the month
4. Keep production effort realistic
5. Suggest angles, not just vague titles

## Default output structure
1. monthly content direction
2. 30 content ideas in table form
3. recommended posting rhythm
4. optional repurposing suggestions
