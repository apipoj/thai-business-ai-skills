# Pain Point Index - Thai Business AI Skills

Use this file to decide what kinds of skills are most worth adding next.

The goal is not to collect clever prompts. The goal is to solve frequent business pain.

## Priority 1: Communication and response work

Common pain:
- ตอบลูกค้าไม่ทัน
- ไม่รู้จะพิมพ์อย่างไรให้สุภาพและมืออาชีพ
- ทีมตอบไม่เหมือนกัน
- ติดตามลูกค้าแล้วกลัวดู hard sell เกินไป

High-value skill types:
- customer complaint reply
- follow-up message
- request more information
- payment reminder
- professional email reply
- internal announcement

Search keywords often related:
- ตอบลูกค้า
- ติดตามลูกค้า
- follow up
- ขอข้อมูลเพิ่ม
- complaint reply
- payment reminder
- email reply

## Decision heuristic

When choosing between multiple possible new skills, prefer the one that:
1. saves repeated time for small teams
2. reduces writing effort or cognitive load
3. improves communication quality
4. helps non-technical users immediately
5. is likely to be reused often
